**Last updated: February 13, 2026**

PrintUpgraded ("we," "us," or "our") operates the PrintUpgraded website. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## What We Collect

We keep things simple. Here's what we may collect:

- **Analytics data.** We use analytics tools to understand how people use the site. This includes things like pages visited, time on site, and referring URLs. This data is anonymous and aggregated.
- **Cookies.** Our site and third-party services (like analytics and ad networks) may place cookies on your browser. These small files help the site work properly and let us understand traffic patterns.

We don't collect your name, email, or any personal information unless you voluntarily give it to us (for example, by contacting us).

## Affiliate Links and Third Parties

Our site contains affiliate links to third-party retailers like Amazon, Overture 3D, Anycubic, Creality, and MatterHackers. When you click these links, those third parties may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We don't control what they collect.

We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

## Display Advertising

We may display ads on this site through third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising through:

- [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)
- [Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)
- [Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out](https://optout.aboutads.info/)

## Your Choices

You can:

- **Block cookies** in your browser settings. Some site features may not work properly without cookies.
- **Use a browser extension** like uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger to limit tracking.
- **Opt out of personalized ads** using the links above.

## Children's Privacy

This site isn't directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
